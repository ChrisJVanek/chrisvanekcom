---
title: "The Banana Diet"
date: "2025-02-28"
excerpt: "A short take on the infamous banana diet and why simple rules sometimes go viral."
---

The banana diet is one of those fads that pops up every so often: eat bananas for breakfast (and sometimes more), and supposedly you lose weight or “reset” your body. It went big in Japan years ago and still gets mentioned whenever people want a single, simple rule to follow.

The appeal is obvious. One food, one rule — no counting, no complicated plans. For a lot of people, that feels manageable. The downside is that any diet built around a single food is going to be narrow in nutrients and hard to sustain. Bananas are fine; they’re not magic.

So if you’re curious: sure, try bananas for breakfast and see how you feel. But treating it as a “diet” with big promises probably isn’t worth it. Better to focus on variety, consistency, and habits you can keep — with or without bananas.
